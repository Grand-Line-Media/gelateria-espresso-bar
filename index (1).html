Our Story

Made by hand.
Made with heart.

Melissa and Dennis started this little shop in 2022 with a big dream: bring the real art of Italian gelato to the desert. They researched the craft together, built the recipes together — and now they scoop, pull shots, and run every shift together too.

Their gelato menu is never static. Flavors rotate weekly, chasing the season and whatever sounds delicious. Vegan options are always on deck. Every scoop is made in-house.
